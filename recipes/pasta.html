Pasta with cheese

Description

This pasta with cheese recipe is a simple and delicious comfort food. Creamy melted cheese coats tender pasta for a quick meal that's perfect for any night. You can use your favorite type of pasta and cheese to customize the flavor.

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  3. Add butter and stir until melted.
  4. Pour in milk and add cheese.
  5. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm.
