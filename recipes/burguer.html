Burguer

Description

This burger recipe is a classic favorite. Juicy beef patties are grilled to perfection and served on a toasted bun with your favorite toppings. Perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  1. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patties until cooked to your liking.
  3. Place cheese slices on the patties and let them melt.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  5. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy.
